B1/B2 INTERVIEW QUESTIONS & ANSWERS

1. What is the purpose of your visit?

"I am visiting my son/daughter who is studying in the USA. He/She is graduating this semester and I want to attend the graduation ceremony."

2. How many days are you planning to stay?

"I plan to stay for 2-3 weeks, just for the graduation ceremony and to spend some quality time with my child."

3. In which state is your son/daughter studying?

"My son/daughter is studying in [State Name], at [University Name]."

4. Who will sponsor your trip?

"I will be sponsoring myself/I have sufficient funds for my trip." OR "My son/daughter will sponsor my visit."

5. What is your occupation and salary?

"I am a [Designation] at [Company Name]. My monthly/annual salary is [Amount]." (Be prepared to show salary slips)

6. Do you have properties in India?

"Yes, I own property in [City]. Here are the documents for your reference." (Show property papers if asked)

7. Have you traveled abroad before?

"Yes, I have visited [Country Names] before." OR "No, this will be my first international trip."

8. Do you have other children?

"Yes, I have [Number] other children living with me in India." (Emphasize family ties in home country)

IMPORTANT TIPS FOR HIGH APPROVAL CHANCE

Answer confidently and truthfully
Show strong ties to home country (property, family, job)
Have all documents organized and ready
Dress formally for the interview
Maintain eye contact and be polite
Practice interview questions beforehand
Carry original documents along with copies
